Why It Matters

Every artist is an entrepreneur.

The music industry was built to extract from independent artists — not empower them. Labels own your masters. Managers take 20%. Nobody explains the contracts. Artispreneur flips the model: you keep ownership, you get the education, you build the infrastructure.

💰

Own Your Royalties

As an independent artist, every stream, sync, and performance generates royalties — but if you're not registered and tracking, that money disappears. We help you claim what's yours.

📜

Know Your Contracts

That contract a label or producer slid you? It probably takes your masters. We break every clause into plain English and give you templates that protect your rights.

🎓

Get the Education

Nobody taught you how to form an LLC, register with a PRO, or negotiate a sync deal. Academy gives independent artists the business education that schools and labels never will.

Build Real Infrastructure
