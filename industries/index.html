The Studio Model

Three ways I create value.

Diamitani Industries is a venture studio and holding company. Some products I build myself; others I co-found with operators; and in some I take a small stake and lend my skills. Every path runs on the same architecture and production discipline.

01 · Build

Built in-house

I design and ship products directly on the ROSTR standard — hybrid AI + deterministic systems, production discipline from day one, and a shared skill library so each new build is faster and safer than the last.

02 · Partner

Venture studio

I co-found and co-build with operators who own a market. They bring the domain and the customers; I bring AI engineering, GTM systems, and the platform — and we split the equity.

03 · Invest

Micro-investments

I back small independent builders with early capital and hands-on help — automation, AI, and go-to-market — taking a modest stake and helping them reach revenue instead of just writing a check.
